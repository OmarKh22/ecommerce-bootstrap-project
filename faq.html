Frequently asked questions

Let us show you how your product customization works.

How does product customization work?

To design a T-shirt, you first need to select your product design choices. Once you've made the selection and the item has been added to your cart, we'll begin printing it exactly as you've designed it.

As soon as your order is placed, our production team will prepare your design for the best fit on your selected product. We'll make sure your design is perfectly positioned and printed with care.

What products can I customize?

You can customize a wide range of products including t-shirts, hoodies, sweatshirts, tank tops, long sleeve shirts, and more. We offer various styles, colors, and sizes to suit your preferences.

Each product has different customization options available, from print locations to special techniques like embroidery on select items.

How are your T-shirts printed?

We use direct-to-garment (DTG) printing technology for most of our custom t-shirts. This advanced technique allows us to print high-quality, detailed designs directly onto the fabric with vibrant colors.

For special orders and bulk quantities, we also offer screen printing which is ideal for simple designs with solid colors. Both methods produce durable prints that withstand multiple washes.

What's the best T-shirt material?

We offer several high-quality materials for our t-shirts. Our most popular options include:

  • 100% Cotton: Soft, breathable, and perfect for everyday wear
  • Cotton/Poly Blend (50/50): More durable with less shrinkage than pure cotton
  • Tri-Blend: Super soft with a vintage feel, made from cotton, polyester, and rayon
  • Performance Fabrics: Moisture-wicking materials ideal for athletic wear

The best material depends on your specific needs and preferences.
